## Problem

In a mobile app onboarding check, there are 6 questions and 4 choices for each question (a, b, c, d). Mina has not reviewed the material at all and decides to randomly guess every answer. (총 6점) What is the probability that

**(a)** the first question she gets right is the 3rd question?

**(b)** she gets exactly 3 or exactly 4 questions right?

**(c)** she gets the majority of the questions right?

----

## Solution

- 각 문항에서 정답을 맞힐 확률은 $p=1/4=0.25$, 틀릴 확률은 $0.75$이다.
- (a) 첫 정답이 3번째라는 것은 처음 두 문항을 틀리고 세 번째 문항을 맞히는 경우이므로
  $$
  0.75^2\times 0.25=0.140625
  $$
- (b) 정확히 3개 또는 4개를 맞힐 확률은
  $$
  P(X=3)+P(X=4)=\binom63(0.25)^3(0.75)^3+\binom64(0.25)^4(0.75)^2
  $$
  $$
  =20(0.015625)(0.421875)+15(0.00390625)(0.5625)
  $$
  $$
  =0.1318359375+0.032958984375=0.164794921875
  $$
- (c) 6문항 중 과반수를 맞힌다는 것은 4개 이상 맞히는 경우이므로
  $$
  P(X\ge 4)=P(X=4)+P(X=5)+P(X=6)
  $$
  $$
  =0.032958984375+\binom65(0.25)^5(0.75)+(0.25)^6
  $$
  $$
  =0.032958984375+0.00439453125+0.000244140625=0.03759765625
  $$
