## 문항

A community wellness team collected data from 132 randomly selected participants to study weekly exercise time. Below are three observations from the data set. The outcome `minutes` is weekly exercise time measured in 10-minute units. Variables are coded as follows: `app` (0 = did not use the coaching app, 1 = used the coaching app), `sex` (0 = female, 1 = male), and `goal` (0 = no written weekly goal, 1 = had a written weekly goal).

|   | app | sex | goal | minutes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 1 | 0 | 1 | 51 |
| 2 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 39 |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |
| 132 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 55 |

The summary table below shows the fitted model output for predicting average weekly exercise time from these three variables.

|   | Estimate | Std. Error | t value | Pr(>|t|) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| (Intercept) | 42.80 | 1.90 | 22.53 | 0.0000 |
| app | 6.25 | 1.55 | 4.03 | 0.0001 |
| sex | -2.40 | 1.48 | -1.62 | 0.1074 |
| goal | 4.10 | 1.50 | 2.73 | 0.0072 |

(a) Write the fitted prediction equation.

(b) Interpret each one of the slopes in this context.

(c) Calculate the residual for the first observation in the data set: a participant who used the coaching app, is female, had a written weekly goal, and recorded 51 units of weekly exercise time.

(d) The variance of the residuals is 38.72, and the variance of weekly exercise time for all participants in the data set is 49.60. Calculate R^2 and adjusted R^2. Note that there are 132 observations in the data set.

----

## 해설

- 예측식은
  $$
  \hat y=42.80+6.25\,app-2.40\,sex+4.10\,goal
  $$
- 각 기울기 해석:
  - `app=6.25`: 다른 변수가 같을 때, coaching app을 사용한 참여자(`app=1`)는 사용하지 않은 참여자(`app=0`)보다 주당 운동 시간이 평균 6.25단위 더 높게 예측된다. 한 단위가 10분이므로 약 62.5분 차이에 해당한다.
  - `sex=-2.40`: 다른 변수가 같을 때, 남성(`sex=1`)은 여성(`sex=0`)보다 주당 운동 시간이 평균 2.40단위 낮게 예측된다.
  - `goal=4.10`: 다른 변수가 같을 때, written weekly goal이 있는 참여자(`goal=1`)는 없는 참여자(`goal=0`)보다 주당 운동 시간이 평균 4.10단위 더 높게 예측된다.
- 첫 번째 관측치는 `app=1, sex=0, goal=1` 이므로 예측값은
  $$
  \hat y=42.80+6.25(1)-2.40(0)+4.10(1)=53.15
  $$
- 실제값이 51이므로 residual은
  $$
  e=y-\hat y=51-53.15=-2.15
  $$
- 분산 정보를 이용하면
  $$
  R^2=1-\frac{38.72}{49.60}\approx 0.2194
  $$
- 설명변수 개수 $p=3$, 표본수 $n=132$ 이므로 adjusted $R^2$는
  $$
  1-(1-R^2)\frac{n-1}{n-p-1}
  =1-(1-0.2194)\frac{131}{128}
  \approx 0.2011
  $$
