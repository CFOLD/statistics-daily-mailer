## 문항

A campus health center screens students for a rare thyroid condition. About 1.8% of students have the condition. For students who have it, the screening test returns a positive result 98.5% of the time. For students who do not have it, the test correctly returns a negative result 92% of the time. If a randomly selected student receives a positive result, what is the probability that the student actually has the condition?

----

## 해설

- Let \(D\) be the event that the student has the condition. Then \(P(D)=0.018\) and \(P(D^c)=0.982\).
- The test detects the condition with probability \(P(+\mid D)=0.985\). Among students without the condition, 92% receive a correct negative result, so the false positive rate is
  $$P(+\mid D^c)=1-0.92=0.08.$$
- The desired probability is
  $$P(D\mid +)=\frac{P(+\mid D)P(D)}{P(+\mid D)P(D)+P(+\mid D^c)P(D^c)}.$$
  Substituting the given values,
  $$P(D\mid +)=\frac{0.985\cdot 0.018}{0.985\cdot 0.018+0.08\cdot 0.982}=\frac{0.01773}{0.01773+0.07856}.$$
  Therefore,
  $$P(D\mid +)=\frac{0.01773}{0.09629}\approx 0.1841.$$
- Thus, even after a positive screening result, the probability that the student actually has the condition is about `18.41%`. The key reason is that the condition is rare, so false positives among students without the condition still make up a large share of all positive results.

## 출제 의도

- 출제의 핵심은 `검사 정확도`와 `실제 양성 확률`이 서로 다르다는 점을 드러내는 데 있다. 학생이 민감도와 특이도를 posterior probability와 혼동하는지 가장 잘 드러나는 영역이다.
- 희귀질환, HIV, 진단장비, 반복 검사 같은 문맥을 주고 prior probability, true positive, false positive를 분리해 쓰게 하는 문항이 반복적으로 나온다.
- 한 번 양성인 경우와 두 번 연속 양성인 경우를 나누어 물으면, 같은 공식 암기 여부가 아니라 정보 갱신의 의미를 이해했는지 확인할 수 있다.
