## 문항

A 2026 survey asked 870 randomly sampled residents of a metropolitan area, "Do you support? Or do you oppose? Expanding protected bicycle lanes in the downtown district? Or do you not know enough to say?" Below is the distribution of responses, separated based on whether or not the respondent had completed a city transportation workshop. (20 점) 참고: `pnorm(1.96) = 0.9750`

|   | Workshop Yes | Workshop No |
|---|---:|---:|
| Support | 170 | 145 |
| Oppose | 185 | 132 |
| Do not know | 105 | 133 |
| Total | 460 | 410 |

1. Use 95% CI to determine if the data provide strong evidence that the proportion of workshop participants who do not have an opinion on this issue is different than that of nonparticipants.
2. Use HT to determine if the data provide strong evidence that the proportion of workshop participants who support expanding protected bicycle lanes is different than that of nonparticipants.

## 해설

- 1) `Do not know` 비율을 비교하면
  $$
  \hat p_1=\frac{105}{460}\approx 0.2283,\qquad
  \hat p_2=\frac{133}{410}\approx 0.3244
  $$
  따라서 차이는
  $$
  \hat p_1-\hat p_2\approx -0.0961
  $$
- 비율 차이의 표준오차는
  $$
  SE=\sqrt{\frac{0.2283(0.7717)}{460}+\frac{0.3244(0.6756)}{410}}
  \approx 0.0303
  $$
- 95% 구간은
  $$
  -0.0961\pm 1.96(0.0303)=(-0.1555,\,-0.0368)
  $$
  이다. 0을 포함하지 않으므로 두 집단의 `Do not know` 비율은 유의하게 다르며, 워크숍 미참여 집단의 무응답 비율이 더 높다.
- 2) `Support` 비율 비교에서는
  $$
  \hat p_1=\frac{170}{460}\approx 0.3696,\qquad
  \hat p_2=\frac{145}{410}\approx 0.3537
  $$
  pooled proportion은
  $$
  \hat p=\frac{170+145}{460+410}=\frac{315}{870}\approx 0.3621
  $$
- 검정통계량은
  $$
  z=\frac{0.3696-0.3537}{\sqrt{\hat p(1-\hat p)(1/460+1/410)}}\approx 0.49
  $$
  이므로 two-sided p-value는 약 `0.626`이다.
- 따라서 protected bicycle lane 확장 지지 비율 차이에 대해서는 강한 증거가 없다고 결론내린다.
