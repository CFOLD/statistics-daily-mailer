## 문항

A training center recorded the completion times (in minutes) for 20 participants in a short data-analysis workshop: 41, 44, 45, 46, 47, 47, 48, 48, 49, 49, 50, 50, 51, 52, 52, 53, 54, 55, 57, 66. Draw a histogram of these data and describe the overall shape of the distribution (5 points).

## 해설

Most completion times are concentrated between 44 and 55 minutes, so the histogram should have a single main peak around the high-40s to low-50s. The value 66 is noticeably larger than the rest and would stretch the right tail, so the distribution is not perfectly symmetric. A good description is unimodal with slight right skew. The center is around 49 to 50 minutes, and the unusually high completion time can pull the mean slightly above the median.
