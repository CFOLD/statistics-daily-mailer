## 문항

A university wants to estimate the proportion of undergraduates who support extending the library to 24-hour service during exam week. In a random sample of 160 students, 56 said they support the plan. (10점)

1. Calculate a 95% interval estimate for the population proportion of students who support the plan.
2. If the university wants the margin of error to be no larger than 4 percentage points at the same confidence level, how large should the sample be?
3. Briefly explain why the required sample size in part 2 is larger than the original sample.

----

## 해설

- The sample proportion is
  $$\hat p=\frac{56}{160}=0.35$$
- The standard error is
  $$
  SE=\sqrt{\frac{\hat p(1-\hat p)}{n}}
  =\sqrt{\frac{0.35\cdot 0.65}{160}}
  =\sqrt{0.001421875}
  \approx 0.0377
  $$
- For a 95% interval, use $z^*=1.96$. The margin of error is
  $$
  ME=1.96\times 0.0377 \approx 0.0739
  $$
- Therefore the 95% interval estimate is
  $$
  0.35 \pm 0.0739 = (0.2761,\ 0.4239)
  $$
  So the population support rate is estimated to be about `27.61%` to `42.39%`.
- For part 2, set the target margin of error to $0.04$ and use
  $$
  n=\frac{z^{*2}\hat p(1-\hat p)}{ME^2}
  =\frac{1.96^2\cdot 0.35\cdot 0.65}{0.04^2}
  =\frac{3.8416\cdot 0.2275}{0.0016}
  \approx 546.54
  $$
- We round up, so the required sample size is `547` students.
- The needed sample is larger because a smaller margin of error means a more precise estimate, and higher precision requires reducing sampling variability by increasing $n$.

- 비율과 평균의 신뢰구간, margin of error, 필요한 sample size 계산은 거의 독립된 출제 축으로 반복된다.
- 문항은 보통 주어진 표본으로 95% 신뢰구간을 구하라, 오차범위를 일정 수준 이하로 줄이려면 표본이 얼마나 필요하냐의 두 단계로 구성된다.
- 좋은 출제는 계산만으로 끝내지 않고, 구간이 무엇을 의미하는지, 신뢰수준을 높이면 왜 구간이 넓어지는지, 표본 수가 왜 오차범위를 줄이는지 문장으로 해석하게 만든다.
