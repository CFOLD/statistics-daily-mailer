## 문항

A hospital is comparing two prediction systems. System A predicts a patient's length of stay in days, while System B predicts whether the patient will be readmitted within 30 days. Describe how the evaluation criteria should differ for System A and System B. (5 points)

----

## 해설

- System A produces a continuous numerical prediction, so its performance should be evaluated by how close the predicted values are to the observed values.
- Appropriate criteria for System A include RMSE, MAE, and, when useful, the proportion of variability explained by the model.
- System B produces a binary decision, so evaluation should focus on how well it separates readmitted and non-readmitted patients.
- Appropriate criteria for System B include accuracy, sensitivity, specificity, precision, ROC/AUC, and the confusion matrix.
- The evaluation metric should therefore be chosen according to the outcome type and the practical cost of different errors. For example, missing a truly high-risk patient may be more serious than incorrectly flagging a low-risk patient.
