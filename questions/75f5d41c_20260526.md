## Problem

A 2026 survey asked 864 randomly sampled employees at large technology companies, "Which workplace arrangement would you prefer for the next year: mostly remote, mostly hybrid, or no clear preference?" Below is the distribution of responses, separated by whether the respondent supervises at least one employee. (20 points) Note: `pnorm(1.96) = 0.9750`

|   | Supervisor Yes | Supervisor No |
|---|---:|---:|
| Prefer mostly remote | 173 | 151 |
| Prefer mostly hybrid | 192 | 118 |
| No clear preference | 97 | 133 |
| Total | 462 | 402 |

1. Use the 95% interval method to determine if the data provide strong evidence that the proportion of supervisors with no clear preference is different from that of non-supervisors.
2. Use the test method to determine if the data provide strong evidence that the proportion of supervisors who prefer mostly remote work is different from that of non-supervisors.

----

## Solution

- 1) For `No clear preference`, compare the two sample proportions:
  $$
  \hat p_1=\frac{97}{462}\approx 0.2100,\qquad
  \hat p_2=\frac{133}{402}\approx 0.3308
  $$
  The observed difference is
  $$
  \hat p_1-\hat p_2\approx -0.1209.
  $$
- The standard error for the difference is
  $$
  SE=\sqrt{\frac{0.2100(0.7900)}{462}+\frac{0.3308(0.6692)}{402}}\approx 0.0302.
  $$
- The 95% interval is
  $$
  -0.1209\pm 1.96(0.0302)=(-0.1800,\,-0.0618).
  $$
  Because 0 is not in the interval, the data provide strong evidence that the two proportions differ. The non-supervisor group has the higher proportion with no clear preference.
- 2) For `Prefer mostly remote`, compare
  $$
  \hat p_1=\frac{173}{462}\approx 0.3745,\qquad
  \hat p_2=\frac{151}{402}\approx 0.3756.
  $$
  The pooled proportion is
  $$
  \hat p=\frac{173+151}{462+402}=\frac{324}{864}=0.3750.
  $$
- The test statistic is
  $$
  z=\frac{0.3745-0.3756}{\sqrt{0.3750(0.6250)(1/462+1/402)}}\approx -0.04.
  $$
  The two-sided p-value is about 0.972.
- Therefore, the data do not provide strong evidence that the mostly-remote preference proportion differs between supervisors and non-supervisors.

## Design Purpose

- 비율과 평균의 신뢰구간, margin of error, 필요한 sample size 계산은 거의 독립된 출제 축으로 반복된다.
- 문항은 보통 `주어진 표본으로 95% 신뢰구간을 구하라`, `오차범위를 일정 수준 이하로 줄이려면 표본이 얼마나 필요하냐`의 두 단계로 구성된다.
- 좋은 출제는 계산만으로 끝내지 않고, 구간이 무엇을 의미하는지, 신뢰수준을 높이면 왜 구간이 넓어지는지, 표본 수가 왜 오차범위를 줄이는지 문장으로 해석하게 만든다.
